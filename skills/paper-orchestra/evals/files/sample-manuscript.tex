\begin{abstract}
We present TSAM, a novel approach for referring audio-visual segmentation. Our method extends the Segment Anything Model with temporal awareness and multimodal prompting capabilities. We achieve state-of-the-art results on the Ref-AVS benchmark.
\end{abstract}

\section{Introduction}

Video segmentation has seen remarkable progress. In our previous work~\cite{smith2024}, we demonstrated that foundation models can be adapted for temporal tasks.
